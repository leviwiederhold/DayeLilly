Rooted in Community

Born from a passion for both aesthetic excellence and local connection, Daye Lily Beauty Bar & Boutique was founded to provide Mount Orab with a high-end destination that feels like home.

We believe that beauty is an experience, not just a service. That's why we've integrated our full-service beauty bar with a carefully curated boutique, offering everything from artisan jewelry to luxury skincare.

Every detail of our space—from the Noto Serif typography on our menus to the soft linen textures of our boutique displays—is designed to make you feel inspired and cared for.
